Interesting Facts About Bears

Did you know that bears are found on every continent except Antarctica?

Bears are omnivores, meaning they eat both plants and animals. Their diet can vary widely depending on their habitat and the availability of food.

The polar bear is the largest species of bear, with adult males weighing up to 1,500 pounds.

Bears have an excellent sense of smell, which they use to find food and detect danger.

Despite their large size, bears are surprisingly fast runners and can reach speeds of up to 30 miles per hour.

Many bear species hibernate during the winter months to conserve energy and survive when food is scarce.
