About

Welcome to Eddie's zoo, where wildlife thrives and conservation matters.

Nestled in the heart of Ghana, our zoo is dedicated to providing a safe and enriching environment for animals from around the globe.

With a commitment to education, research, and conservation, we strive to inspire our visitors to connect with nature and become stewards of the planet.

Come explore our diverse habitats, engage with our knowledgeable staff, and embark on a journey of discovery as you learn about the fascinating creatures that call Eddie's zooo home.
