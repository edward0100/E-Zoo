Interesting Facts About Sharks

Did you know that sharks have been around for over 450 million years?

Sharks have incredible senses, including the ability to detect electric fields produced by other animals.

Contrary to popular belief, not all sharks are apex predators. Some species feed on plankton and are harmless to humans.

The largest shark species, the whale shark, can grow up to 40 feet in length.

Sharks play a vital role in maintaining the health of ocean ecosystems by controlling populations of other marine animals.

There are over 400 different species of sharks, ranging in size from the tiny dwarf lanternshark to the massive whale shark.
