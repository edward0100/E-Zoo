All About Cheetahs

Cheetahs: The Fastest Land Animals

Cheetahs are known for their incredible speed and agility. They can reach speeds of up to 70 miles per hour!

Fascinating Facts about Cheetahs:

  • They have distinctive black spots on their golden coats.
  • Cheetahs are excellent hunters, relying on their speed to catch prey.
  • Unlike other big cats, cheetahs cannot roar.
  • They are primarily found in Africa.
