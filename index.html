Meet Our Wild Animals!

Lion

Lions are the most fiercest wild cats, mostly found in Africa.

Cheetah

Known for high speeds, Cheetahs are the fastest land mammals.

Wolf

Wolves are known for hunting in packs.

Antelope

Antelopes are known for their agility, found mostly in Africa.

Snake

Snakes are among the most dangerous animals on earth.

Bear

Bears are the biggest members of the dog family.

Fox

Foxes are small but agile animals, hunting mostly rabbits.

Shark

Sharks are fierce hunters in the oceans with powerful bites.

Spider

Spiders are arachnids, known for their ability to spin webs.
